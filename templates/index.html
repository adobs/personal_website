OKC+

Ever heard of the dating site OkCupid? OKC+ takes OkCupid to the next level with innovative analytics and tools. The OKC+ PostgreSQL database contains 58K profiles that were scraped from OkCupid.

Users can explore the most commonly used adjectives from these profiles on a Google map, and filter results by 22 genders, 12 orientations, and a 80+ year age range.
Alternatively, users can discover how different portions of peopleâ€™s profiles cluster based on machine learning using a mean shift clustering algorithm, displayed with interactive D3 and Chart.js visuals.

Users can create an OkCupid account on the app, or login with their existing OkCupid credentials to access additional features, such as a messaging bot and a profile generator that uses Markov Chains.
